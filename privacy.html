Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hjalta Fitness & Wellbeing (“Hjalta”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website www.hjalta.com or interact with us through our services, assessments, or communications.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

a) Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number (e.g., when filling out forms or booking consultations)

  • Health or fitness-related information if voluntarily submitted during consultations or assessments

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

All third parties are required to respect the confidentiality and security of your data.

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your data is stored securely on servers that comply with international data protection standards.

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
