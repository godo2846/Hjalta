About Hjalta Fitness & Wellbeing

Hjalta embodies Nordic strength - derived from the Shetland term for the hilt of a sword, representing balance, control, and unwavering foundation in movement and mindset.

Established in the UAE, our integrated approach combines elite personal training with mindfulness practices and mobility science. We serve discerning clients who seek comprehensive transformation - physical mastery paired with emotional resilience and mental clarity.

"We built Hjalta to redefine fitness excellence. Beyond physical training, we cultivate sustainable wellbeing - where strength meets serenity, and performance aligns with purpose."

— Edward G. Fullerton, Founder
