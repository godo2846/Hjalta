About Hjalta Fitness and Wellbeing

Hjalta was born from Nordic roots — inspired by the Shetland word meaning the hilt of a sword, a symbol of strength, balance, and control. What began as a passion for movement and mental clarity became a mission to help others feel stronger from the inside out.

Now based in the UAE, Our Hjalta blends personal training with mindfulness, mobility, and emotional wellbeing. We’re here for those who want more than just fitness people who value calm, connection, and purpose.

"I started Hjalta Fitness & Wellbeing because I wanted to help people feel stronger and happier. Just like I do when I work out... and make it a little easier and more enjoyable for everyone." — Edd, Founder
